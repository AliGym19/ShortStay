## 1. Details of processing (Art. 28(3) — subject matter, duration, nature, purpose)

- **Subject matter:** guest and booking personal data stored in your ShortStay account.
- **Duration:** the term of your subscription plus the 30-day export window.
- **Nature:** storage, organisation, retrieval, display, syncing to Xero on your instruction, deletion.
- **Purpose:** providing the ShortStay back office and integrations to you.

- **Data subjects:** your guests and enquirers; your staff users.
- **Personal data:** names, contact details, booking dates and property, payment status, communications, notes you record, identity document references where you collect them for legal reasons.
- **Special category data:** not intended. If a guest volunteers accessibility or dietary information and you record it, you confirm you have an Article 9 condition for doing so.

## 3. Our commitments as processor

1. **Documented instructions only.** We process guest data only on your documented instructions — which are: these terms, your configuration of the platform, and actions you take in the app (including triggering a Xero sync). We'll tell you immediately if we believe an instruction infringes UK GDPR or other data protection law.
2. **Confidentiality.** Everyone we authorise to access guest data is bound by confidentiality obligations.
3. **Security.** We apply appropriate technical and organisational measures: encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, audit logging, tested backups, and documented security procedures.
4. **Sub-processors.** You give general written authorisation to the sub-processors listed at [DOMAIN]/subprocessors. We'll give 30 days' notice before adding or replacing one, and you may object on reasonable data protection grounds (your remedy if we can't resolve it: terminate and export). Sub-processors are bound by equivalent written terms and we remain liable for them.
5. **Data subject rights.** We provide in-app tools (search, export, delete) to help you answer access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability and objection requests, and will assist further where the tools don't cover it. Requests reaching us directly from your guests get forwarded to you without undue delay.
6. **Breach and assistance.** We'll notify you without undue delay after becoming aware of a personal data breach affecting guest data, with enough detail for your own ICO notification, and will assist with your security, breach notification, DPIA and prior consultation obligations.
7. **Deletion or return.** At the end of the contract you choose: export your data during the 30-day window, or ask us to delete sooner. After the window we delete all guest data, except where law requires retention. Backups roll off within [35] days.
8. **Demonstrating compliance and audits.** We'll make available the information reasonably necessary to demonstrate compliance with Article 28, and allow and contribute to audits — normally satisfied by our security documentation and sub-processor list; on-site audits require 30 days' notice, once per year, at your cost, and must not compromise other customers' data.
